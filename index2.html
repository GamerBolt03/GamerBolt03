Our Story

We wanted to make a new innovative shoe and were thinking how we can make school shoes less boring. The answer we came to was to make the shoe colorful, but most schools don't allow colored shoes. So we thought: what if it could switch between colors from basic black or white to colored? Then we had to think of a way to actually change the color of the shoe, so we did some research and found electrochromic fabrics that change color using electricity.
